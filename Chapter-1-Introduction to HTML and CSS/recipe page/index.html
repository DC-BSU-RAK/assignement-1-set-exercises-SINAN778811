Porotta and Beef Roast

Porotta and Beef Roast is a popular South Indian delicacy, especially loved in Kerala. The soft, flaky porotta pairs perfectly with spicy, slow-cooked beef roast that’s rich in flavor and aromatic with traditional spices.

Ingredients

  • 4 Kerala porottas (store-bought or homemade)
  • 500g beef, cut into small cubes
  • 2 large onions, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons coconut oil

Method

  1. Pressure cook the beef with turmeric, salt, and a little water until tender. Set aside.
  2. Heat coconut oil in a large pan. Add curry leaves and sliced onions, and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until raw smell disappears.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft and oil starts to separate.
  5. Add chili powder, coriander powder, and garam masala. Cook the masala until well blended.
  6. Add the cooked beef along with its stock. Mix well and roast on medium heat until dry and the meat is well coated with spices.
  7. Serve hot with layered Kerala porottas. Enjoy the spicy and flavorful combo!
