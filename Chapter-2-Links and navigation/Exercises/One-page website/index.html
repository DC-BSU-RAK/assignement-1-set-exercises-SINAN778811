Stranger Things

*Stranger Things* is a science fiction horror series set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana. The story begins with the mysterious disappearance of a young boy and follows a group of friends as they uncover secret government experiments, supernatural forces, and a girl with psychokinetic abilities named Eleven.

Key Information

  • Genre: Sci-Fi, Horror, Thriller
  • Created by: The Duffer Brothers
  • Network: Netflix
  • First Aired: July 15, 2016
  • Seasons: 4 (Season 5 coming soon)

Cast & Creative Team

Main Cast:

  • Millie Bobby Brown as Eleven
  • Finn Wolfhard as Mike Wheeler
  • David Harbour as Jim Hopper
  • Winona Ryder as Joyce Byers
  • Gaten Matarazzo as Dustin Henderson
  • Caleb McLaughlin as Lucas Sinclair
  • Sadie Sink as Max Mayfield

Key Characters

Eleven: A girl with supernatural powers, escaping a government lab.

Mike: Loyal friend and group leader with a soft spot for Eleven.

Hopper: The town's police chief drawn into the Upside Down mystery.

Joyce: A determined mother searching for her son against all odds.
